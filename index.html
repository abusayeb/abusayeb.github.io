01 — Research

Research interests

Working toward intelligent autonomous systems that perceive, reason about and act in the physical world.

My work centres on robot perception, embodied AI and intelligent autonomous systems. In practice that means computer vision that holds up outside the benchmark — multi-object tracking, human activity recognition and pose estimation on messy real-world video — combined with multimodal vision-language models and generative models for simulation and robotic learning. I am particularly interested in how generative simulation can close the data gap for embodied agents.

Robot Perception
Multi-object tracking, pose estimation and activity recognition for agents operating in unstructured environments.
Multimodal VLMs
Vision-language models for grounded scene understanding and instruction following.
Generative Simulation
Diffusion models for synthetic data generation, simulation and robotic learning at scale.
